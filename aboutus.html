THE MOST EXCITING AND TRENDING DESIGN TEMPLATES THE WORLD HAS TO OFFER YOU

Combadi has been created with one simple mission in mind: To research and select the most intriguing and transformative travel ideas that you could experience.

Our vision is to motivate you to discover your life's passion, learn or discover something new—to simply have the time of your life. No matter where you travel, the goal of Combadi, is to differentiate from what you have experienced so far and guide you to discover a new side of you.

That's it: an honest approach to help you Come Back Different from your next holiday.

Combadi is a member of the Travel Trust Association which is a respected travel trade association that exists in order to protect you, the customer, with 100% financial protection on package holidays. This means that when you book directly with Combadi every penny that you pay to Combadi for a package holiday is protected by the Travel Trust Association.

Why working with us?

If you are wondering why you should book with us, here is a list of reasons that will convince you.

HANDPICKED HOLIDAYS

Holidays are chosen and vetted carefully by our—hard to please—team. This is why all the travel experiences handpicked to be featured on Combadi, will be really far/different from your usual holiday experience.

NO MARK-UP, GUARANTEED

Combadi’s price promise: The price you will find on our website is the price that the provider is charging. No fine print, no gimmicks, no markup. So simple.

LOCAL PROVIDERS

All holidays are offered by authentic local providers, who are passionate about what they do. We are eager to represent only providers that focus on what they do best—aiming to create great experiences. Our holidays are like hidden gems, in a world of large global travel operators and we want to ensure you find out about them.
